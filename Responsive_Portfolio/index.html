About Me

Bryan is first and foremost a visual artist. After earning his BFA in Studio Art from The School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 1998 he moved back to California to pursue a career in traditional film animation. During that time there was a downturn on traditional animation opportunities since animation started going digital. Not having qualifications in computer animation he decided to go into film production, working on various films as a PA, sound engineer, and assistant directing.

Currently, he is working as an Uber driver and also trying to get his wedding videography business Life Footage off the ground, and is enrolled in the UCLA Extension Fullstack web development program, while trying to get his face into the "music industry" as they say.
